Crispy Garlic Fried Chicken Wings

This is a simple recipe for making delicious Crispy Garlic Fried Chicken Wings at home.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Wings
  • Egg
  • Cornstarch
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Cooking Oil

Marinade Ingredients:

  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Paprika
  • Garlic
  • Ground White Pepper
  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce

White Sauce Ingredients:

  • Mayonnaise
  • Sour Cream
  • Dill
  • Parsley
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Kosher Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Lime

Steps:

  1. Combine the marinade ingredients in a bowl.
    1. Add the chicken and mix well to coat every piece.
    2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Make the ranch sauce.
    1. Combine all sauce ingredients in a bowl and mix thoroughly. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the breading and coat the chicken.
    1. Gradually add cornstarch and flour to the chicken.
    2. Add a cracked egg and mix until the chicken is well coated.
  4. Fry the chicken wings.
    1. Heat 2 cups of cooking oil in a wok to 350 °F. Fry the chicken wings one at a time.
    2. Set heat to medium and fry for 8–10 minutes, or until golden brown.
    3. Remove the chicken from the oil.
  5. Let the wings rest, then double fry.
    1. Let the chicken cool for 10–12 minutes.
    2. Return the chicken to the wok and fry for 1 minute to make it extra crispy.
  6. Serve with ranch sauce.

And that’s it! Place the chicken on a serving plate and serve with ranch sauce.
